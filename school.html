School

We organized a p4est summer school sponsored by the Hausdorff Center for Mathematics at the University of Bonn, Germany. Please see also the school's home page at HCM.

We gratefully acknowledge support by the Bonn Hausdorff Center for Mathematics funded by the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG). Thanks to all co-organizers and speakers! Additional thanks to Tim Griesbach for writing the forest tutorial and to Inga Trost for postprocessing the sound on most of my 2020 lecture videos.

The online event July 20th–24th, 2020, is now over.

The tutorials/exercises linked below are still being updated.

We will keep adding material on this site, possibly:

  • Text tutorials, presentation videos
  • Exercises and tasks for self-study
  • Demo programs and solutions

During the actual week of the conference, we have been hosting video tutorials and Q&A sessions during German afternoon time and discussed selected solutions to excercises and best practices.

You may find the recordings of Big Blue Button sessions in the webpage of the p4est school room.
